Mingqi Yuan

Trinity College, Cambridge, 2024.

I’m a Ph.D. candidate of Department of Computing, Faculty of Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (HKPU), under the supervision of Prof. Wenjun Zeng (Fellow, IEEE and CAE) and Prof. Bo Li. In 2024, I was a visiting student at the University of Cambridge, under the supervision of Prof. Fumiya Iida. In 2022, I received the M.Phil. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Man-on Pun. Before that, I received the B.S. degree from Minzu University of China (MUC) in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Jing Li.

In addition, I had worked at Tencent Robotics X Lab and JD.com, Inc. as a research intern in 2022 and 2019, respectively. My research interests are reinforcement learning, robotics and computer vision.

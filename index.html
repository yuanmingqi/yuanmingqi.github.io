Mingqi Yuan

I’m a Ph.D. candidate of Department of Computing, Faculty of Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (HKPU), under the supervision of Prof. Wenjun Zeng (Fellow, IEEE) and Prof. Bo Li. In 2022, I received the M.Phil. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Man-on Pun. Before that, I received the B.S. degree from Minzu University of China (MUC) in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Jing Li.

In addition, I had worked at Tencent Robotics X Lab and JD.com, Inc. as a research intern in 2022 and 2019, respectively. My research interests are reinforcement learning, robotics and computer vision.
